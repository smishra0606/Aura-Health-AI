How to Get Better Results

  • →Be Specific: Instead of "feel sick," describe the symptoms like "sore throat and mild cough."
  • →Include Duration: Mention how long you've had the symptoms (e.g., "for 3 days").
  • →Note Severity: Use words like 'mild,' 'sharp,' or 'severe' to describe pain or discomfort.
  • →Add Context: Include any relevant medical history or recent activities if you feel they are related.

Seek Immediate Help For...

This tool is 🚨NOT🚨 for emergencies. Go to the nearest hospital if you experience any of the following:

  • - Severe chest pain or pressure
  • - Difficulty breathing
  • - Sudden numbness or weakness
  • - Loss of consciousness
  • - Uncontrolled bleeding
  • - Severe headache or confusion
